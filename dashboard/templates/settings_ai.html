AI Module Guide
Overview

The AI Analysis Module uses Large Language Models (LLMs) to analyze market sentiment from news and social media. It generates a sentiment score (-1 to +1) which can be used to trigger automated trades.

Modes
  • Support Tool: (Direct Trading Disabled) The module only logs sentiment scores and provides insights on the dashboard. No trades are executed.
  • Direct Trading: (Direct Trading Enabled) The module executes BUY orders when sentiment is strongly positive and meets your confidence threshold. In DRY RUN mode, these are simulated.
Configuration
  • Confidence Threshold: The minimum sentiment strength required to trade. Recommended: 80%+.
  • Trade Amount: Fixed USD amount per AI-triggered trade.
