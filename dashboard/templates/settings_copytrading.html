Copy Trading Guide
Overview

The Copy Trading Module monitors specific "smart money" wallets and automatically replicates their buy/sell transactions on your account.

Setup
  • Target Wallets: Add the Ethereum/Solana addresses of successful traders you want to copy.
  • Copy Ratio: Determines your trade size relative to theirs. If a whale buys 10 ETH and your ratio is 10%, you buy 1 ETH (limited by Max Copy Amount).
Risk Warning

Copy trading involves significant risk. The wallets you copy may engage in strategies that are high-risk or specific to their portfolio size. Always set a strict Max Copy Amount.
